WHO AM I?

My name is Filip but you can call me Flip as it's easier to pronouns. I'm years old and I was born in Kalmar, a small city in southeast Sweden. I graduated from university summer 2022 where I studied game programming at Blekinge Institute of Technology.

I have worked on games by myself and with groups up to 10 people. I'm interested in 3D graphics, skeletal animation and optimizing code for best performance. I feel most comfortable working with the programming language C++.
